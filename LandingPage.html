The ERR Eatery was first established in year 2017 right along barangay Dos Hey Street Period in Cagayan de Oro City in front of Lourdes College. Our main customers are mostly students from Lourdes College but that only means that they only come every Monday to Friday to buy food and our main goal is to attract more customers but in year 2020, the ERR Eatery transferred place in NHA Kauswagan because their location at Barangay Dos Hey Street is prone to flooding. And now, it has been a year since it opened in their new location and our regular customers are taxi drivers, construction workers, passerby and etc. Our Eatery has actually many specialties but our best selling is Dinugoan, because of it we attract more customers and people loves it. This eatery are owned by husband and wife Arlene and Reymond Segador. They both named their eatery after their daughters names which are Erika, Rhea, Rhika. That's why their eatery stands for ERR.
